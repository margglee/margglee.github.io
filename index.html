Michigan Weather App

Made my first app!, Sept 8, 2019

I made my first app after doing an Android workshop about sensors. Using that knowledge, I applied it to my parody weather app. Since Michigan natives always joke about the rapidly changing weather, I wanted an app that captures the unpredictability. My app randomly choses one of the five weather statuses and each weather icon does a different action. Some statuses use sensors but every page is a lot of fun!


Taylor Swift Lyrics Analysis

Interesting Swift Lyrics Information, Aug 27, 2019

For my Coursera capstone project, I scraped the web for Swift's lyrics using Python and visualized it using JavaScript. Ignoring stop words and other words that I determined to not hold much meaning, I found that in every album, "know" is in the top 10 words used. I found this to be interesting because it's not a word I would associate with Swift, but looking back, she does declare that someone ought to "know" something often. Another interesting fact is that she never uses any variation of "woman" in her songs, even in her newest album, released at age 30. She still uses variations of "girl", so it seems that she has not grown up yet. Variations of "boy" and "man" are used throughout every album.
